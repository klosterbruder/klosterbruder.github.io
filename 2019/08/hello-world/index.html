Hello, World!

Hello, World!

For some years now, I thought I should start a blog. Either because everyone and their grandma has one, or because I had exactly one more or less nonsensical idea I could’ve thrown out onto the internet. Being lazy as heck was the main deterrent of actually doing it, the other one was a mindset of having to do it “right” on first try. I’m not even sure what “right” means in the context of blogging, so those attempts didn’t last much longer.

Recently, a friend told me:

It’s better to push out something ‘crappy’ then nothing at all.

So, err, yea. Here we are.

I am a tech guy, a Sysadmin. I can also do a bit of programming, but I’m far from good at it. The topics covered in my posts will (mainly?) be tech-related as well. They are published in hopes someone will find them funny, interesting or even helpful, but without any expressed or implied guarantees of being successful or even non-harmful. But you wouldn’t curl | sudo bash anything unchecked from elsewhere on the internet either, right? Right?!

This blog is made with Hugo, which is a fast static website generator written in Go. At the moment, I’m using the Solar theme. Both theme and site structure may be subject to change, though, as I’m still experimenting a lot with Hugo.
