New year, old me

The year 2019 has gone just as 2020 came, ringing in the decade of the (hopefully) golden ‘20s once again (see also: XKCD 2249). All the while I realized that I have shamefully neglected the blog I started on a whim, even though actually having several topic ideas to write about. I have no excuse, I’m just lazy like that. Getting from “Oh, I could write about that!” to an actual blog post that’s not just an incoherent string of thought fragments always seems like way too much of a pain in the rear department for the precious free minutes between youtube, random webpages and my warm bed.

I will try to better myself, though. It’s not really a resolution for the new year, but I will strive for releasing one post every month over a 10-year average. Whether that means about 100 twitter-lenght posts of muddled sentences in december 2029 will remain to be seen. Thinking realistically, it’ll be the latter.

Until next post, I wish you a happy and healthy 2020.
