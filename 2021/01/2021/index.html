2021

The year 2020 is over. It’s 2021. And after a…time, I’ve finally set out to actually complete a blog post again.

A…time, yes. 2020 seemed so long, and at the same time so short. So much happened, and yet almost nothing did. The news were full of ever-changing situations, and yet it was the same over and over again. The arrival of Christmas was an actual surprise for me, I have to admit. Working from home was comfortable, but despite the additional time, I got little additional stuff done. The things I did get done were putting my PC into a workable state and thereby migrating back from Ubuntu to Gentoo. A few of my planned and/or started posts are about interesting experiences I had along the way. Taking a bit more or better care of my blog is, again, a plan for this year.

Speaking of my blog. I fixed a few spelling mistakes in old posts, and at the same time went from Hugo 0.58 to 0.78. Regenerating the static pages got me this:

19 files changed, 83 insertions(+), 230 deletions(-)

Whoa. Ain’t that a tad much?

Until next post, I wish you whatever you wish for yourself and those dear to you. “Happy and healthy” didn’t work out that well last year, after all.
